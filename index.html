Community First

Nova 2.0 — The Noven Fork

24 phases of protocol-level innovation. Solving Ethereum's biggest problems. Battle-tested on a live devnet. Now live on mainnet (v2.0.0 — Noven Fork at block 480,000).

🛡 Native Security

Anti-Reentrancy built into the EVM. No more DAO hacks, no more Curve exploits. Every contract is protected by default.

Gas Refund on Revert — failed transactions return 90% of gas. On Ethereum you lose everything.

⚖ Anti-MEV

Transactions ordered by arrival time, not gas price. No front-running. No sandwich attacks. Fair for every user.

Rate limiting prevents mempool spam. Miners cannot reorder for profit.

♻ State Expiry

The blockchain garbage collector. Dead contracts automatically archived after 1,000 blocks. Keeps the chain lean forever.

Your wallet is NEVER expired. Only abandoned contracts get cleaned up.

🪙 Native Tokens

Tokens as protocol objects — no ERC-20 contracts needed. 10x cheaper transfers. No approve+transfer phishing pattern.

🔐 Optional Privacy

Shielded pool for private NOVA transfers. Your choice when to be private. Not forced like Monero. Native in protocol.

📊 Native Oracle

Protocol-level price feeds with TWAP. No Chainlink dependency. Circuit breaker blocks manipulation.

Open Technical Deep Dive

📦 Account Abstraction

Smart Wallets with guardian recovery and key rotation. Lost your keys? Guardians recover your account.

Tempo Transactions — atomic batching (approve + swap in ONE tx), fee delegation (DApps pay gas for you), and scheduled transactions.

Frame AA — smart contract wallets validate transactions. Supports passkeys, multisig, and quantum-resistant signatures.
